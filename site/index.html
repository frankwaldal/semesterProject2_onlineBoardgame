Climb to the Mist Mountain - an online boardgame

Local Play

Play against a friend at the same location, in the same browser.

Online Play

Play online against other players, in seperate browsers.

About this game

Goal of the game

The goal of the game is to reach the top of the Mist Mountain first.

On the way there you might meet scary monsters that you will have to fight to get to the top of the mountain.

Rules

  • A dice throw decides who starts the game.
  • Rolling a 6 gives you an extra turn.
  • If you land on a battle tile, you get 6 dices, and you need to roll the required rolls to stay on the tile. If you loose your rolls, you will be sent back 2 tiles.
  • The first to pass the goal tile wins.
